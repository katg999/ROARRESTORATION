About Us

Her body. Her voice. Her roar. Roar Restoration is a youth-led initiative that aims to revolutionise access to health information and services for young people in Uganda. To provide mental health suppourt, stigma-free abortion care and reproductive health information to young people in Uganda. We provide sexual education to Ugandan girls and adolescents impacted by early pregnancy centering their voices in advocacy and care

Why Choose Roar and Restoration

We are dedicated to providing compassionate, safe, and accessible abortion care services throughout urban and rural communities in Uganda, leveraging technology and community engagement to overcome healthcare barriers.

Comprehensive Healthcare Services

We provide complete reproductive healthcare with trained medical professionals who prioritize your wellbeing and dignity throughout your healthcare journey.

AI-Powered Telemedicine Bot

Our innovative AI-powered telemedicine bot provides 24/7 access to information, initial consultations, and guidance, breaking down barriers to reproductive healthcare in both urban and rural settings.

Confidential and Safe Environment

We ensure complete privacy and security for all patients, creating a safe space for those seeking our services with strict confidentiality protocols and protective measures.

Reaching Rural Communities

We bridge the critical gap in healthcare access by bringing essential reproductive services to underserved rural areas across Uganda, where access to proper care is often limited or non-existent.

Our Team

Dedicated staff Team led by our Chief Executive Officer Charity Atuheire and a team of passionate volunteers, we are committed to revolutionising healthcare initiatives and abortion is at the fore front of our mission. Our team is made up of a diverse group of individuals who are passionate about making a difference in the lives of young people in Uganda.

About Us

Roar & Restoration stands as a pioneering non-governmental organization committed to transforming reproductive healthcare in Uganda by providing comprehensive, accessible, and dignified abortion care services to women and girls across both urban centers and remote rural communities. Established in response to the critical gaps in reproductive healthcare access, our organization combines evidence-based medical practices with innovative digital solutions, including our AI-powered telemedicine platform, to overcome geographical, economic, and social barriers that have historically prevented vulnerable populations from accessing essential care. We operate through a network of trained healthcare professionals, community health workers, and mobile clinics that bring services directly to underserved regions, while simultaneously advocating for policy reform and destigmatization of reproductive health services.
